Challenges

100 Babies Challenge

The 100 Babies Challenge is a challenge in which you have to start out with a matriarch (A female Sim preferably a young adult) who will have as many children as they can in there lifetime. Some of the rules are you can't have multiple babies with the same person, you can't have a job, you have to do specific tasks to be able to age up the children. Legacy Challenge

You will start out with a young adult Sim. You will then move them into a 50x50 lot. Then start your Sim with only 1800 Simoleons. Your goal is to get married, have children and then play with the family for 10 generations on that one lot. If you want all the rules click the link below. Full Rules
